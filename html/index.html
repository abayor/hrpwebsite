Hospital Record Portal (HRP) is an Electronic Medical Record (EMR) software application built to manage your patients medical records and to offer them the best personalized patient-centered care in the hospital from the point of entry till healthcare service is delivered.

HRP is a product of Infinite MediFix Solutions Limited, a software development company with multi-faceted group of young achievers disrupting the healthcare industry in Nigeria with innovative technology software solutions for use by individuals, hospitals, Laboratories and other health related parastatals, promoting the speed of access to quality health care.

BENEFITS

SAVE TIME

HRP saves hospitals more time to deliver the best medical care patients’ deserve by organizing their work process flow so they don’t miss a thing.

SAVE MONEY

No more cost-intensive expenditures on printing papers. Hospitals can keep track of the cash and stock inflow and outflow with our bespoke accounting and pharmacy inventory modules.

GET SECURED

With HRP only authorized hospital staff have access to patient data/records and no loss of records to negligence, fire outbreaks, water spillages, etc.
